Reflection

Personal Insights

This project allowed us to further explore experience design with the added challenge of incorporating a physical product with a digital product. We had to consider multiple touchpoints and constantly had to adjust our methods in order to create a seamless experience. My main take-away from this project was realizing the importance of creating persona’s to better understand who you are designing for. I have never surfed in my life before, but through my research I was able to distinguish what information would actually be relevant to a surfer. Going forward I will continue to create persona’s so that my design decisions are valuable to the end user.
