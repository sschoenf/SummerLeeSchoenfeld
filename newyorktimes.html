Business Opportunity

Stated in Journalism That Stands Apart, The New York Time’s goal is to increase digital revenue from $500 million to $800 million by the 2020, so that they can fully support themselves through their digital model without relying on their physical print-first strategy.

Pitch

New York Times

We are proposing a digital service that is provided alongside the New York Time’s subscription program for senior high school students that delivers a new format for long-form journalism; in order to increase New York Time’s digital subscriptions and improve media literacy among young readers.

During this touchpoint where students are interacting with the new york times for school, we have an opportunity to develop an experience that can help earlier cement better news consumption habits while they search for news topics for school. The specific habits we are trying to promote are :

Informing students on how to evaluate background and sources to validate journalistic credibility

Promoting engagement with long form content to better understand details and context

Ultimately encourage exploring a different sources to view a variety of perspectives on a topic so that they are not reliant on one source
